What is EasyLink?

EasyLink is a community-focused Wi-Fi hotspot provider based in Arua City, Uganda. We are dedicated to bridging the digital divide by offering reliable and affordable internet access to residents, students, businesses, and visitors. Our network utilizes strategically placed hotspots, allowing users to connect easily using prepaid vouchers available from local agents and shops.

We believe that access to information and communication is a fundamental need in today's world. EasyLink aims to empower the Arua community by making the internet accessible and affordable for everyone, fostering education, business growth, and social connection.

Our Mission

To provide affordable, reliable, and community-first Wi-Fi access throughout Arua City, ensuring everyone has the opportunity to connect, learn, and grow.

Our Vision

To expand quality internet access across Northern Uganda, becoming a leading force in digital inclusion and community empowerment through connectivity.

Our Core Values

  • Community Focused: We prioritize the needs of our local community in everything we do.
  • Integrity: We operate with transparency and honesty, building trust with our users and partners.
  • Innovation: We embrace new technologies and ideas to continuously improve our services.
  • Sustainability: We are committed to environmentally friendly practices in our operations.

Our Goals

We aim to:

  • Expand our network to cover more areas in Arua and beyond.
  • Enhance user experience with faster speeds and better connectivity.
  • Foster partnerships with local businesses and organizations to promote digital literacy.
  • Innovate our service offerings to meet the evolving needs of our users.

Our Story

EasyLink was founded by Emmanuel Wani B.J, a passionate entrepreneur with a deep understanding of the connectivity challenges faced by the Arua community. Witnessing the high costs and limited availability of reliable internet, Emmanuel envisioned a solution that was both affordable and accessible to the average person.

Starting with a single hotspot near Arua Primary School, EasyLink was born out of a desire to make a tangible difference. The focus was simple: create a user-friendly system using vouchers that local shops could easily sell, making internet access as common as buying airtime. Through dedication and community support, EasyLink has steadily grown its network and user base, driven by the core belief that connectivity fuels progress.

Emmanuel's commitment to digital equity didn't stop at Arua. He envisioned a broader impact, one that empowers underserved areas with tools to bridge the digital divide. This meant training local vendors, partnering with schools, and introducing flexible data packages that work for everyone—from students and small businesses to entire families.

Under his leadership, EasyLink has become more than just a Wi-Fi provider—it's a catalyst for digital transformation. Emmanuel's ability to blend technical expertise with grassroots insight has enabled EasyLink to become a trusted name in connectivity. His journey continues to inspire local entrepreneurs to dream bigger and build solutions tailored to their communities.

As EasyLink expands across Northern Uganda, our mission stays rooted in simplicity, service, and sustainability. We're building not just networks, but opportunities—for education, innovation, and economic growth.
