April 28, 2025

Easy Link Partners with Arua Primary School for Digital Literacy Program

We're proud to announce our partnership with Arua Primary School to support their new Digital Literacy Program, providing free internet access for educational purposes.

As part of our commitment to community development, Easy Link has installed a dedicated hotspot at Arua Primary School and will provide free internet access for the school's computer lab and administrative offices. This initiative aims to enhance digital literacy among students and support teachers in accessing online educational resources.

The program includes:

  • Free Wi-Fi access during school hours for educational purposes
  • Basic computer and internet training for teachers
  • Special discounted vouchers for students to use outside school hours
  • Regular digital literacy workshops for the community

"Access to the internet is increasingly essential for education in today's world," said Emmanuel Wani Bongo Julius, founder of Easy Link. "We're excited to support Arua Primary School in their efforts to prepare students for a digital future."

The headteacher of Arua Primary School expressed gratitude for the partnership, noting that it will significantly enhance the learning experience for students and provide teachers with valuable resources for lesson planning and research.

This initiative is part of Easy Link's broader commitment to supporting education and digital inclusion in Arua City. We plan to expand the program to additional schools in the coming months.

New Hotspot Location: Ediofe Cathedral Area Now Live

We're pleased to announce that our newest hotspot location in the Ediofe Cathedral area is now operational, expanding our coverage to serve more community members.

The new hotspot is strategically located at the community center near Ediofe Cathedral, providing strong coverage throughout the surrounding area. This expansion is part of our ongoing efforts to increase internet accessibility across Arua City.

The Ediofe hotspot features:

  • High-capacity equipment to serve multiple users simultaneously
  • Strong signal strength covering approximately 100 meters in all directions
  • Full support for all Easy Link voucher packages

Vouchers for the new hotspot are available at several nearby shops, including Ediofe General Store and St. Augustine Bookshop. Look for the Easy Link logo displayed at these locations.

We're excited to serve the Ediofe community and welcome your feedback on this new location. Please contact us with any questions or suggestions.

March 20, 2025

Easy Link Celebrates 1,000 Active Users Milestone

We're thrilled to announce that Easy Link has reached a significant milestone: 1,000 active users across our network in Arua City!

Just eighteen months after launching our first hotspot near Arua Primary School, we're proud to have connected over 1,000 community members to affordable internet access. This achievement reflects the growing demand for reliable connectivity in our community and the trust our users place in Easy Link.

To celebrate this milestone, we held a small ceremony at our office in Mvara Trading Center, attended by partners, resellers, and some of our earliest customers. During the event, Emmanuel Wani Bongo Julius, founder of Easy Link, shared the company's journey and vision for the future.

"When we started Easy Link, our goal was to make internet access affordable and accessible for everyone in Arua," said Emmanuel. "Reaching 1,000 active users is a testament to the community's need for this service and their support of our vision. We're just getting started, and we're excited about our plans to expand coverage and improve services in the coming months."

As part of the celebration, we recognized our top-performing reseller partners and gathered valuable feedback from users to guide our future improvements.

We extend our sincere gratitude to all our users, partners, and supporters who have been part of this journey. We remain committed to our mission of providing affordable, community-first Wi-Fi access throughout Arua City and beyond.

Here's to the next 1,000 users and beyond!
