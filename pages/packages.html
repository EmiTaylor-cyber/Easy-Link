Fair Usage Policy

At Easy Link, we strive to provide the best possible internet experience for all our users. To ensure fair access for everyone, we implement a Fair Usage Policy across all our packages:

  • All packages provide access to the full internet without site restrictions.
  • During peak hours (6 PM - 10 PM), bandwidth may be optimized to ensure all users have a good experience.
  • Continuous high-bandwidth activities (like large downloads or HD video streaming) may experience temporary speed adjustments during peak hours.
  • Monthly Unlimited users receive priority during peak hours.
  • The use of peer-to-peer file sharing applications is discouraged as they can negatively impact network performance for all users.

Our Fair Usage Policy is designed to prevent a small number of users from consuming excessive bandwidth that would affect the experience of others. We do not impose hard data caps, but rather manage the network to ensure everyone gets a fair share of the available bandwidth.

If you have specific bandwidth requirements for business or special purposes, please contact us to discuss custom solutions.
