Paying to Connect to EasyLink using MoMo Payment

Follow these easy steps to get online with EasyLink Wi-Fi using Mobile Money. The process is quick and straightforward, requiring just a few taps on your phone.

1. Choose Your Package

Select the internet package that suits your needs. Options for different time periods include (7 hours, 24 hours, 7 days, etc.).

Visit our Packages page to view all available options and pricing.

2. Turn On Wi-Fi & Select EasyLink Network

Enable Wi-Fi on your device (phone, laptop, tablet) and look for the "EasyLink" network in your list of available networks.

Ensure you're within range of an EasyLink hotspot. The signal is strongest within 100 meters of a hotspot location.

3. Connect to the Network

Tap or click on the "EasyLink" network to connect. No password is required for the initial connection.

If the login page doesn't appear automatically, open your browser and try to visit any website. You should be redirected to the login page.

4. Make Payment via Mobile Money

On the login page, select your desired package and choose your preferred Mobile Money payment method (MTN, Airtel, or EasyPay). Follow the on-screen instructions to complete the payment.

Ensure you have sufficient funds in your Mobile Money account before initiating the payment.

5. Start Browsing

Once your payment is confirmed, you'll be connected to the internet! You can now browse, stream, download, and enjoy all your online activities.

Your session will remain active for the duration of your selected package. If you disconnect, you can reconnect using the same device until the session expires.

Mobile Money Payment Option

Easy Link supports various Mobile Money platforms for your convenience.

MTN Mobile Money

Use the MTN MoMo app or dial *165# to make payments directly from your MTN Mobile Money account.

Airtel Money

Use the Airtel Money app or dial *185# to make payments directly from your Airtel Money account.

EasyPay Wallet

Use the EasyPay app or dial *270*26# to make payments. EasyPay supports cross-network mobile money transfers and bill payments.

Note: After a successful Mobile Money payment, the system will automatically connect you. If not, refresh your browser or return to the login page to retry.

Troubleshooting Connection Issues

Having trouble connecting? Here are solutions to common issues:

Login Page Not Appearing

  • Try opening your browser and visiting any website
  • Clear your browser cache and cookies
  • Try using a different browser
  • Disconnect and reconnect to the Wi-Fi network

Code Not Accepted

  • Ensure you used the correct Mobile Money code or number
  • Check if the payment was successfully sent and confirmed
  • Wait a few moments — delays can occur during peak hours
  • If you're not automatically connected, refresh the page or retry login
  • Still stuck? Contact Easy Link support with your payment reference

Slow Connection

  • Move closer to the Wi-Fi hotspot or router
  • Avoid peak times when many users are connected
  • Restart your phone or device
  • Pause or close apps that use a lot of internet (e.g. video streaming)

Disconnected Unexpectedly

  • Confirm your session duration (4Hrs, 8Hrs, or 24Hrs) has not ended
  • Reconnect to the Easy Link Wi-Fi and retry logging in
  • Make sure your Mobile Money payment was successful
  • Clear your browser cache or try a different browser
  • Contact support with your payment ID if the problem continues
