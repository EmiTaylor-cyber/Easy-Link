Coverage FAQs

What is the typical range of an Easy Link hotspot?

Each Easy Link hotspot typically provides reliable coverage within a 100-meter radius in open areas. However, actual coverage may vary depending on physical obstacles, building materials, and other environmental factors. For best performance, we recommend staying within 50 meters of a hotspot.

How can I request a new hotspot in my area?

We're always looking to expand our network! If you'd like to request a hotspot in your area, please contact us through our contact form or speak with one of our agents. Business owners can also explore our partnership program to become a hotspot host.

Can I use my Easy Link voucher at any hotspot location?

Yes! Your Easy Link voucher works at any of our hotspot locations throughout Arua City. Once activated, you can move between different hotspots within our network during your active session without needing to log in again.

How often do you add new hotspot locations?

We aim to add new hotspot locations monthly, focusing on areas with high demand and community needs. Our expansion plans are guided by user requests, population density, and strategic partnerships with local businesses. Check our News page for announcements about new locations.
