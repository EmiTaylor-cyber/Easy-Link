How Can We Help You?

What is Easy Link?

EasyLink is a community-focused Wi-Fi hotspot provider based in Arua City, Uganda. We offer affordable internet access through strategically placed hotspots across the city. Users can connect using prepaid vouchers available from local shops and authorized agents.

How do I get started with EasyLink?

Getting started is simple:

  1. Purchase an EasyLink voucher from any authorized reseller in Arua
  2. Turn on your device's Wi-Fi and connect to the "EasyLink" network
  3. When the login page appears, enter your voucher code
  4. Start browsing!

For more detailed instructions, visit our How to Connect page.

Where can I buy EasyLink vouchers?

EasyLink vouchers are available from various authorized resellers throughout Arua City, including:

  • Local convenience stores and shops
  • Market kiosks
  • Authorized EasyLink agents

Look for the EasyLink logo or ask for EasyLink vouchers at these locations. You can also check our Coverage page to find resellers near hotspot locations.

What devices can connect to EasyLink Wi-Fi?

EasyLink is compatible with any Wi-Fi-enabled device, including:

  • Smartphones (Android, iPhone)
  • Laptops and computers
  • Tablets
  • Smart TVs
  • Gaming consoles

Any device that can connect to a standard Wi-Fi network can use EasyLink services.

Why did my code expire early?

Voucher codes are valid for the specified time period from the moment of activation, regardless of whether you're actively using the connection. For example, a 24-hour voucher will expire exactly 24 hours after activation, even if you disconnect during that period.

If you believe your code expired earlier than it should have, please contact our support team with your voucher code details for assistance.

How can I reconnect after disconnecting?

If you disconnect from Easy Link Wi-Fi and need to reconnect during your active session:

  1. Ensure your Wi-Fi is turned on
  2. Select the "EasyLink" network again
  3. Open your browser - you should be automatically reconnected if your session is still active
  4. If prompted for a code, enter your original voucher code

Note: If your voucher time has expired, you'll need to purchase a new voucher to continue browsing.

Wi-Fi is slow — what should I do?

If you're experiencing slow connection speeds, try these troubleshooting steps:

  1. Move closer to the hotspot location
  2. Ensure there are no physical obstacles between your device and the hotspot
  3. Try connecting during off-peak hours (early morning or late evening)
  4. Restart your device's Wi-Fi by turning it off and on again
  5. Clear your browser cache

Connection speeds may vary based on the number of users connected to the same hotspot, distance from the hotspot, and network conditions. If problems persist, please contact our support team.

The login page isn't appearing. What should I do?

If the login page doesn't appear automatically after connecting to Easy Link:

  1. Open your browser and try to visit any website - you should be redirected to the login page
  2. Try typing 1.1.1.1 in your browser address bar
  3. Clear your browser cache and cookies
  4. Try using a different browser
  5. Restart your device and try connecting again

If none of these solutions work, please contact our support team for assistance.

Can I pause my voucher time when not using it?

Currently, voucher time cannot be paused once activated. The countdown begins from the moment you first log in and continues regardless of whether you're actively using the connection. For example, a 24-hour voucher will expire exactly 24 hours after first use, even if you disconnect during that period.

We recommend activating your voucher when you plan to use it for an extended period to maximize your usage time.

Can I use my voucher on multiple devices?

Each voucher code can only be used on one device at a time. If you try to connect with a second device using the same code, the first device will be disconnected.

If you need to switch devices during your active session (for example, from your phone to your laptop), you'll need to disconnect from the first device before connecting with the second one using the same voucher code.

What if my voucher code doesn't work?

If your voucher code isn't working, check the following:

  1. Ensure you're entering the code exactly as it appears on your voucher (codes are case-sensitive)
  2. Verify that the code hasn't been used before (each code is valid for one-time activation)
  3. Check that your voucher hasn't expired or been damaged
  4. Make sure you're connected to the "Easy Link" network

If you've checked all these points and still have issues, please contact our support team with your voucher details for assistance.

Do you offer refunds for unused vouchers?

We generally do not offer refunds for purchased vouchers. However, if you experience significant technical issues that prevent you from using our service, please contact our support team with details of the problem and your voucher code.

Each case will be evaluated individually, and we may offer a replacement voucher or other solution depending on the circumstances.

Where are Easy Link hotspots located?

Easy Link hotspots are strategically placed throughout Arua City, with concentrations around:

  • Arua Primary School area
  • Arua Central Market
  • Arua Airfield
  • Arua Dadamu area

For a complete and up-to-date list of all hotspot locations, please visit our Coverage page.

How far does the Wi-Fi signal reach?

Each Easy Link hotspot typically provides reliable coverage within a 100-meter radius in open areas. However, actual coverage may vary depending on:

  • Physical obstacles (buildings, walls, trees)
  • Building materials (concrete, metal)
  • Environmental factors
  • Device capabilities

For best performance, we recommend staying within 50 meters of a hotspot in an open area.

Can I use Easy Link at home?

Easy Link is primarily designed as a public Wi-Fi hotspot service. You can use it at home if:

  1. Your home is within range of an existing Easy Link hotspot
  2. You become a partner and host a hotspot at your location

If you're interested in hosting a hotspot at your home or business, please visit our Become a Partner page for more information.
