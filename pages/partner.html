How the Partnership Works

1

Apply to Become a Partner

Fill out the application form below with your business details and contact information. Our team will review your application and get in touch with you.

2

Receive Voucher Stock

Once approved, you'll receive your initial stock of Easy Link vouchers to sell. We use a simple consignment model - you only pay for what you sell.

3

Sell Vouchers to Customers

Display the Easy Link partner sign at your location and start selling vouchers to customers. Each voucher has a unique code that customers use to access our Wi-Fi network.

4

Earn Commissions

Earn commissions on every voucher sold. For every 30 tickets sold, you receive 3 free vouchers that you can sell or use yourself. The more you sell, the more you earn!

5

Restock and Grow

When your voucher stock runs low, simply contact your Easy Link representative for a restock. As your sales increase, you may qualify for higher commission rates and additional benefits.

Frequently Asked Questions

Is there any cost to become a partner?

No, there is no upfront cost to become an Easy Link reseller partner. We provide vouchers on a consignment basis, meaning you only pay for what you sell. For hotspot host partners, we provide and install the necessary equipment at no cost to qualified businesses.

How do I get paid for voucher sales?

Partners keep their commission from each sale. For example, if a voucher sells for UGX 1,500 and your commission is UGX 200, you collect UGX 1,500 from the customer and remit UGX 1,300 to Easy Link. Additionally, for every 30 vouchers sold, you receive 3 free vouchers that you can sell at full price or use yourself.

What are the requirements to host a hotspot?

To qualify as a hotspot host partner, your business should:

  • Be located in a high-traffic area or community hub
  • Have reliable electricity or backup power
  • Have secure premises for equipment installation
  • Commit to promoting Easy Link services
  • Have a good reputation in the community

Our team will assess your location during the application process to determine suitability.

How often will I need to restock vouchers?

Restocking frequency depends on your sales volume. Typically, partners restock weekly or bi-weekly. We have a simple process for requesting new voucher stock - just contact your Easy Link representative when your supply is running low, and we'll arrange delivery or pickup of new vouchers.

What support does Easy Link provide to partners?

Easy Link provides comprehensive support to all partners, including:

  • Initial training on voucher sales and customer service
  • Marketing materials (signage, posters, flyers)
  • Regular visits from Easy Link representatives
  • Technical support for hotspot host partners
  • Dedicated WhatsApp group for quick communication
  • Regular updates on new features and promotions

Our success depends on your success, so we're committed to supporting our partners every step of the way.
